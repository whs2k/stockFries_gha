What are Hedge Funds Shorting?

Inspired by the great work by investors from r/WallStreetBets we're publishing all shorts from our list of Hedge Funds (based on their most recent filings). Data is calculated by (1) starting with the "Data by Options" from the footer, (2) removing stocks where there is a "Put" and "Call" position from a fund (i.e. those that are using options actually for hedging), and (3) grouping by "Company" and sorting by "Value."
